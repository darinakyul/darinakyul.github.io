ABOUT OUR COMPANY


Founded in 2019 Vaya Tov provides both business and leisure travellers with luxury serviced accommodation. Our impeccable & cosy home away from home offers our guests the privacy and comfort a hotel simply couldn't.


Make yourself at home in one of our stylish places, which are available for short- and long-term stays.


This is achieved using the rent-to-rent model, where we provide landlords with guaranteed rent every month while taking on the management of the property. We do renovation where needed and make sure to highlight the natural charm and beauty of the place.



Good interior design is what we excel at, each of our locations is uniquely styled with bespoke artworks, luxe bed linen and of course all necessary amenities like high-speed WiFi.


We want to build long term relationships with our clients and partners, that’s why we pay great attention to the demands of each individual..


It all starts with a conversation. If you are interested and want to learn more just give us a call today and we’ll be happy to discuss the details!
